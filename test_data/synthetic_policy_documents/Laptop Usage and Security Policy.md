# Laptop Usage and Security Policy

## 1. Purpose

This policy establishes guidelines for the safe, responsible, and compliant use of company-issued laptops and IT devices. Its goal is to protect company data, maintain operational security, and ensure consistent usage standards across the organization.

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, interns, and third-party users who have access to company laptops, regardless of location.

## 3. Responsibilities of Users

* Maintain the laptop in good working condition.
* Ensure the device is used primarily for company work; personal use must remain minimal and compliant.
* Regularly update all required security and software patches.
* Immediately report performance issues or security concerns to the IT Department.

## 4. Security Requirements

* **Password Security:** Use strong passwords, never share credentials, and enable multi-factor authentication when available.
* **Device Locking:** Systems must lock automatically after inactivity and must be manually locked when leaving the workstation.
* **Data Protection:** Sensitive documents must be stored only in approved company systems, with encryption enabled.
* **Remote Work Security:** When working remotely, employees must use a secure Wi-Fi network or connect via the company VPN.
* **Travel Security:** Laptops must not be left unattended in public locations or vehicles.

## 5. Software and System Restrictions

* Only IT-approved software may be installed.
* Disabling antivirus, firewalls, monitoring agents, or security tools is strictly prohibited.
* Connecting external storage devices requires prior approval from IT.

## 6. Prohibited Activities

Employees may not:

* Use the laptop for illegal or unethical activities.
* Access or store inappropriate, offensive, or copyrighted content without authorization.
* Attempt to modify internal system configurations or bypass security protocols.

## 7. Loss, Theft, or Damage

* Any loss or theft must be reported within **24 hours**.
* The employee must cooperate fully with IT and, if necessary, law enforcement.
* Replacement or repair decisions will be made by the IT Department.
