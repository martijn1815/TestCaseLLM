# Communication & Meeting Policy

## 1. Purpose

This policy outlines expectations for professional communication and meeting conduct across the company to ensure efficiency and clarity.

## 2. Scope

Applies to all internal and external communication conducted on behalf of the company.

## 3. Communication Standards

* All communication should be clear, respectful, and concise.
* Employees must use official communication channels for work-related matters.
* Sensitive information may only be shared via secure methods.

## 4. Email Guidelines

* Use professional language and avoid unnecessary CCs.
* Respond to internal emails within 1 business day unless otherwise noted.
* Mark urgent messages clearly using approved indicators.

## 5. Meeting Expectations

* Schedule meetings only when necessary and include clear agendas.
* Start and end meetings on time.
* Document meeting notes and action items and share them promptly.
* Virtual meetings require cameras on unless otherwise agreed.

## 6. Meeting Etiquette

* Avoid multitasking during meetings.
* Mute when not speaking.
* Be respectful of differing opinions.
