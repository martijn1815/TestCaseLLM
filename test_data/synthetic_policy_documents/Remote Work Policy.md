# Remote Work Policy

## 1. Purpose

This policy provides guidelines for employees working remotely to maintain productivity, collaboration, and data security while ensuring a healthy workâ€“life balance.

## 2. Scope

Applies to all employees approved for partial or full remote work arrangements.

## 3. Remote Work Expectations

* Employees must be available during core business hours unless otherwise agreed with their manager.
* Work output, communication, and responsiveness should match onsite performance standards.
* Employees are responsible for maintaining a suitable and safe home-working environment.

## 4. Communication

* Teams must use company-approved communication tools.
* Daily or weekly check-ins may be required depending on the nature of the role.
* Employees must keep calendars updated to reflect availability.

## 5. Equipment and Security

* Employees must use company-provided equipment for all work-related activities.
* Access to the company network must be secured using VPN.
* Confidential documents should not be printed or stored at home unless strictly necessary.

## 6. Performance and Compliance

* Remote employees must meet all performance expectations.
* Managers may require periodic onsite presence when needed.
